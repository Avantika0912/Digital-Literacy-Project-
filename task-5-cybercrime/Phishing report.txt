During this research on cybercrime, I have learned that cybercrimes are increasing day by day as more and more people are using the internet for communication and other activities. Cybercrimes like phishing and UPI scams are more serious because they involve sensitive information. In most cases, cybercriminals send fake messages and emails to users and trick them into sharing sensitive and confidential information like passwords and OTPs.

During this research, I have also learned that lack of knowledge is one of the main reasons behind cybercrimes. Users do not have proper knowledge about the source and activities of cybercriminals. This shows the importance of knowledge in this field.

During this research, I have also learned that some preventive measures can be taken to avoid cybercrimes. One should always keep a strong password and enable two-factor authentication. One should avoid using public Wi-Fi for any kind of transaction. It is also important to report cybercrimes to the authorities as soon as possible by contacting helpline number 1930 and the official cybercrime portal.
